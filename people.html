Grant W. Brown, Ph.D.
el jefe


Publications
Google Scholar

Grant received his Ph.D. in Molecular Biology from UCLA where he worked in Dan S. Ray's lab studying DNA replication in the trypanosomatid C. fasciculata. His postdoctoral fellowship was at the Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine in Baltimore with Tom Kelly, where he studied regulation of S phase in fission yeast. He has been at U of T since 1999, Associate Professor since 2004, and Professor since 2011. Donnelly Centre member since 2005. Instructor of the CSHL Yeast Genetics Course since 2014. Canada Research Chair in Genome Integrity.
